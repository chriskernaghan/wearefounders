eveningDesk is building an email-first support inbox for e-commerce sellers, aimed at replacing the mix of support email, store admin tabs, and spreadsheets small teams end up juggling. Support email lands in one focused inbox, and connecting WooCommerce surfaces order status, items, and shipping on the relevant ticket automatically instead of requiring a manual lookup.
 
AI drafts replies using order context and store knowledge, but every message goes through human review before it sends. Team features cover assignment, collision detection, canned responses, and a full audit trail, and a live chat widget can pull website visitor messages into the same inbox.
 
The product is currently in early access rather than generally available. Joining the waitlist doesn't create a payment or a workspace, and the pricing shown, starting around €20/agent, is described as the intended post-pilot direction rather than a live charge. You'll be able to see and accept the final plan before anything is billed.
