CompeteDesk answers a specific question: when a buyer searches for you online, or asks ChatGPT or Perplexity to compare options, do you actually show up? It checks Google, Google AI Overview, ChatGPT, and Perplexity against the buyer questions people ask before they book a call, then shows exactly where a competitor appears instead, with the source page that's shaping the answer.

Rather than a generic visibility score, the output is up to three buyer loss cards, each with cited proof, a search-demand check so you know if the lost query is worth chasing, and one prioritized fix you can hand to whoever owns your site. A before-and-after retest is included once the fix ships.

It's a fixed-scope, one-time £295 sprint rather than a subscription, and the site is direct about where it doesn't apply: pre-launch products, consumer apps, and companies without clear named competitors. A free preview checks three buyer questions before you commit to the full sprint.
