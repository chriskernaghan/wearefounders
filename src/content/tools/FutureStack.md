FutureStack is a community-driven directory for discovering and comparing AI tools. Where most directories are static catalogs, FutureStack adds a community layer: every tool carries upvotes, reviews, and comments from people who actually use it, so you can gauge quality without leaving to search elsewhere.

Discovery is organised around who you are rather than what a tool does. Students, developers, freelancers, and marketers each get a feed tuned to their use case, which makes browsing faster than category-by-category hunting.

Listing is free and permanent for tool makers at every tier, a deliberate decision rather than a launch promotion. The Stack of the Day feature gives daily winners an embeddable badge that links back to their listing.

FutureStack is early stage and being built in public. The catalog grows daily and the community features are live, but it is not a finished product, and it does not yet drive the volume of the largest established directories.
