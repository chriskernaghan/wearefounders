Yield Theory is a monthly research letter for self-directed investors, combining macro analysis, stock research across 100+ tickers, congressional trade tracking, and a free set of investing tools in one place.

Research is source-led: filings, disclosures, and transcripts are cited, with assumptions and risks stated explicitly and what would change the thesis called out. A free research archive, investing glossary, and calculators (compound interest, DCA, CAGR) sit alongside the paid monthly letter.

**What it does differently:**
- Combines macro research, stock analysis, congressional trades, and calculators in one place
- Cites sources and states assumptions and risks up front
- Explains investing concepts in plain English
- Offers a free research archive and tools alongside the paid monthly letter

**Not a good fit for:** day traders, guaranteed-return seekers, or anyone looking for personalized financial advice. This is research and education for self-directed investors, not a signals service or an advisor.

Educational only; not personalized investment advice.
