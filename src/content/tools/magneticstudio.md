Magentic Studio audits your processes with AI and hands back a roadmap built around how your organization actually works. Most AI recommendations lean on commonly felt pains, which biases the advice toward the median organization. Magentic Studio tailors its roadmap to your specific processes and workflows instead.

The honesty is part of the pitch. When AI would not actually fix the underlying issue, the system says so rather than recommending it anyway.

For teams, it goes wider. Magentic Studio maps the full cartography of the organization and finds correlations between inefficiencies sitting in different departments, giving you a clear read on the status quo versus the ideal scenario.

It is a good fit if you do not have the time to dive deep into AI but want to be part of the shift. It is less suited to highly technical users or anyone already embedded in the AI world, who will likely want more control than a guided audit provides.

Paid plans start at 4.99 dollars a month, and there is a free tier to test it first.
