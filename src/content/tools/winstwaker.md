Winstwaker is online bookkeeping software for Dutch entrepreneurs that combines a self-serve portal with accountant support, rather than leaving founders fully on their own. It covers the practical side of Dutch administration, document upload, invoices, VAT returns, annual accounts, payroll administration and switching from a previous accountant.

The model is fixed-price monthly bookkeeping with your current figures available in one portal. Where integrations exist you can connect your systems directly, and upload any remaining documents securely when automation is not available.

It is built specifically for Dutch tax and bookkeeping workflows. It is not a free DIY accounting app, not an enterprise ERP or consolidation suite, and not intended for companies needing accounting outside the Netherlands.

Pricing starts at EUR 29 per month for an eenmanszaak, EUR 39 for a VOF or maatschap, and EUR 49 for a BV. Operated by Winstwaker B.V. (KvK 84193891), based in Zeist.
