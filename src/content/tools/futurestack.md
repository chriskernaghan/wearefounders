FutureStack is a community-driven directory for discovering and comparing AI tools. Every tool carries upvotes, reviews and comments from people who actually use it, so you can gauge quality without leaving to search elsewhere.

Discovery is organised around who you are rather than what a tool does. Students, developers, freelancers and marketers each get a feed tuned to their use case.

Listing is free and permanent for tool makers at every tier. The Stack of the Day feature gives daily winners an embeddable badge that links back to their listing.

FutureStack is early stage and being built in public. The catalog grows daily and the community features are live, but it does not yet drive the volume of the largest established directories.
