ProbationWatch replaces the probation spreadsheet with a single timeline for UK employers. Add each new starter and it automatically calculates review milestones from their start date, so you always know who needs a check-in and when a decision is due, instead of chasing dates across calendars and inboxes.

Reviews use structured prompts for objectives, feedback, support, and outcomes, and each record can be sealed as a PDF export with a SHA-256 fingerprint for a defensible paper trail. It's built for UK employers with roughly 5 to 50 staff, not as a full HR suite, so there's no payroll, holiday management, or SSO.

It's explicit about its limits: ProbationWatch organizes dates and records, it doesn't decide whether a dismissal or extension is lawful, and the site points employers toward qualified HR or legal advice for case-specific decisions. Two active probation employees are free with no card required, and Founding Pro is £19/month after a 30-day trial.
