Sequenzy is an AI-native email marketing tool aimed at founders who want automation without the bloat or price tag of legacy platforms. Email creation and sequencing are built around AI, and a Stripe integration makes it straightforward to tie email to customers and revenue.

Pricing is the other pitch: paid plans start at 19 dollars a month, well below most of the field, and there is a free tier to test it first.

It is built for product, lifecycle and revenue email, not cold outreach. It is not intended for cold email campaigns or MLM-style sending, and it does not currently carry SOC 2 or similar certification, so teams with hard compliance requirements should look elsewhere.
