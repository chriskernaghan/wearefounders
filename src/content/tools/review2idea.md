Review2Idea takes a public App Store or Google Play link and turns the negative reviews into structured research. Paste a competitor's URL and it clusters repeated complaints, scores them for severity, and surfaces the strongest patterns alongside the real review text that backs them up.

The output goes beyond a pain point list. Each cluster gets turned into interview questions, a narrow opportunity hypothesis, and PRD notes you can act on, so you walk away with something closer to a research brief than a spreadsheet of quotes.

It's deliberately cautious about what the data can prove. A repeated complaint tells you where to look, not that people will pay to fix it, and the product pushes you toward real customer interviews before you commit to building anything. The first 200-review scan is free with no card required; Pro is $19/month for 200 credits, roughly 8 deep 500-review scans.
