Navor is an AI accounting platform built specifically around Swiss requirements: VAT statements, QR-bill invoicing, and CHF handling, rather than a generic package with Switzerland bolted on. It covers document capture, bank statement import and reconciliation, invoicing, and partner data in one workspace, and it's designed for both individual Swiss SMEs and fiduciaries managing multiple client companies.

What sets the workflow apart is that it doesn't require learning new accounting software. You can send documents and ask questions through ChatGPT, Claude, WhatsApp, or email, and the Navor Agent handles classification, extraction, and reconciliation behind the scenes while surfacing results for review before anything posts.

Data stays hosted entirely in Switzerland, with zero-retention AI processing and FADP compliance, and Navor is already working with the Canton of Valais on digitizing accounting dossiers. The platform supports English, German, French, and Italian. Pricing starts at CHF 59.90/month with a 14-day free trial and no free tier.
