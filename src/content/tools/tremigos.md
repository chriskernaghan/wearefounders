Tremigos is an AI-driven suite for teams that need to communicate across languages, covering live interpreting, document translation, transcription, captions, and dubbing from a single workspace. Live Sessions handles real-time captions and AI interpreting for online, onsite, and hybrid meetings across Zoom, Teams, Meet, browser, and room capture, in more than 60 languages.

Media Studio generates captions, dubbing, and cloned voice outputs for video and audio projects with review-ready exports, while the Documents workflow translates PDFs, DOCX, PPTX, XLSX, and text files while preserving layout, structure, and terminology.

It's built for teams with an ongoing multilingual communication need, rather than as a one-off consumer translator. Founder Eray Dinler leads Tremigos as CEO, with a free tier available and paid plans for teams that need more.
