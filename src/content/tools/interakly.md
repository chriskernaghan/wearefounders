Interakly turns a normal YouTube video, or your own upload, into something a viewer actually has to engage with. Paste a link or upload a file up to 40 minutes long, then drop in questions, polls, matching, or hotspots at the exact timestamp they matter. Playback pauses for graded knowledge checks, gives instant feedback, and viewers watch and answer without ever needing an account.

Every session gets tracked automatically. The analytics dashboard shows completion rates, per-question breakdowns, score distributions, and time spent, exportable as CSV, so a tutor or trainer can see exactly where viewers got stuck without asking. Uploaded videos also support branching, letting one video adapt to each viewer's choices for training simulations or onboarding.

The founder built Interakly after his wife, a Spanish tutor on Preply, wanted her YouTube lessons to pause for comprehension checks instead of playing straight through. It's free during early access, with unlimited uploads and no credit card required to start.
