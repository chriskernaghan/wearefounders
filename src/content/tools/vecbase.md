Vecbase gives every agent its own secure sandbox, persistent files, and real tools, built to complete multi-step work rather than stop at a chat response. Create specialist agents for research, writing, engineering, or operations, and each task runs in a cloud environment with its own working files rather than losing context between messages.

Agents can run on a schedule, a daily 9am market brief or a Monday investor update, without anyone needing to remember to ask. They connect to the tools a team already uses, sending Gmail, writing Notion docs, opening GitHub PRs, or posting in Slack, and everything they produce, reports, spreadsheets, slide decks, images, lands in a shared team library everyone can access.

Pricing is subscription plus usage-based credits rather than a free tier. Plus is $20/month with $20 of included usage credits, and Pro is $50/month with more credits and persistent 24/7 agents. It's built for teams comfortable reviewing agent output on important actions, not for someone who just wants an occasional one-off chatbot answer.
