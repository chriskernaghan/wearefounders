DemandThread turns repeated Reddit pain points into source-backed opportunity research. Instead of another mention-monitoring dashboard, it delivers a done-for-you decision brief: original buyer quotes with source links, the failed workarounds people are already using, and one concrete messaging or offer test to try next.

A free daily feed and a $19/mo Pro tier support DIY research across followed subreddits. For a market-specific answer, the $99 custom report is delivered in 24-48 hours with 10-30 source quotes, buyer context, and a 7-day validation plan. Agencies can commission the same research white-labeled for client work.

**What it does differently:**
- Delivers a done-for-you decision brief instead of another monitoring dashboard
- Includes original public source links and buyer quotes for every pattern
- Turns repeated language into one concrete messaging or offer test
- Delivers custom research in 24-48 hours
- Supports white-label delivery for SaaS agencies

**Not a good fit for:** regulated medical, legal, or financial decisions; private-community research; markets with very little public Reddit discussion; or teams that only want automated mention monitoring without analysis.

A full sample report is available at [demandthread.com/sample-report](https://demandthread.com/sample-report), and the agency partnership option at [demandthread.com/for-agencies](https://demandthread.com/for-agencies).
