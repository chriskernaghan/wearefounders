FleetStart Radar watches three official UK data sources, Traffic Commissioner operator licence applications and decisions, the CQC registration directory, and DfT Street Manager roadworks, and turns them into commercial signals for teams that sell to fleets. Rather than a raw feed, each signal comes with a plain-English reason it might be worth researching, a timestamp, and a link back to the source record for verification before anyone acts on it.

Beyond monitoring, the workspace adds account research, notes, pipeline status, scoring, CSV export, and daily or weekly digest options, so a signal turns into a workflow rather than another feed to check manually. JobGuard cross-references saved customer jobs against live roadworks to flag access risk before a scheduled visit, and CQC search doubles as a live directory alongside change alerts.

The site is direct about its limits: it's not telematics, route-planning, or compliance software, it doesn't contact prospects on your behalf, and a public record isn't treated as proof of budget or intent. Coverage currently launches in the West Midlands, with other regions to follow based on demand. Founding pricing is £59.99/month for the first 20 customers, rising to £99.99/month afterward, and a free weekly Fleet Growth Pulse email is available to try the signal quality first.
